I'm currently a third year PhD student in Xiamen University, supervised by Prof. Xinghao Ding. Before that, I got the B.Eng degree in Communication Engineering at Zhengzhou University in 2014. Now I am doing the visiting scholar research supervised by Prof. John Paisley in the Data Science Institute at Columbia University.

My research interests include medical image reconstruction, medical image analysis and machine learning.
